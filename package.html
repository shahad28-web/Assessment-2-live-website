Day by Day

Your week, mapped out

Day 1

Arrive & Breathe

Direct flight from Heathrow, seaplane transfer over the atolls, welcome cocktail, and your first sunset from the villa deck. It starts immediately.

Days 2–3

Reef Life & Lazy Afternoons

Morning snorkelling — reef sharks, turtles, eagle rays. Afternoons: kayak, hammock, or spa. Evenings: fine dining over the water.

Days 4–5

Island Hop & Spa Day

Boat trip to a local island, private sandbank picnic, and your complimentary 60-minute spa on day five. Completely at your own pace.

Days 6–7

Dolphin Cruise & Goodbye

Sunset dolphin-watching cruise with champagne on the penultimate night. Final morning: one last sunrise, a long breakfast, and back to the seaplane.
